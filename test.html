GARDEN OF EDEN

Ayubowan! (May you live long)

There are only a handful of places on earth where you may wake up to the calls of a tropical wild, and that too on an uncharted coastline. At the edge of Sri Lanka’s most popular national park, this experience is all too familiar in a natural habitat we have shared with wildlife for centuries, and now with the world for centuries more

Welcome to Garden of Eden!

ON THE OUTSKIRTS OF YALA NATIONAL PARK 
Beautifully positioned between the serene tranquility of the Indian Ocean and the natural wonders of Yala National Park, Jetwing Yala rests within an uncharted patch of coastal wilderness at the end of Sri Lanka’s southern shoreline. With convenient accessibility to both the road and the sea, our home of Sri Lankan hospitality remains comfortably connected amidst the coastal wilds of our tropical island.
